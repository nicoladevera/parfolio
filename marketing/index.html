FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

What is the PAR format? ▼

PAR stands for Problem-Action-Result. It's a proven framework for structuring behavioral interview answers that clearly demonstrates your impact.

What can I upload to my Career Memory? ▼

You can upload resumes, LinkedIn profile exports, articles you've written, and transcripts from past work. Our AI indexes everything for semantic search.

Is my data private and secure? ▼

Yes. Your stories and documents are stored securely with Firebase and are never shared or used to train AI models. Only you can access your data.

What if I'm not good at speaking off the cuff? ▼

That's exactly why PARfolio exists! Ramble, pause, restart — our AI is designed to work with messy, natural speech. You don't need to be polished; that's our job.
